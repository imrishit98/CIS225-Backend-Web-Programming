Program description:

Our program gives our users the chance to compare their thoughts and opinions with the rest of society, by asking multiple choice questions then showing the percent of people that choose each answer.

Who is our user?
Anybody (with internet)
Target Audience
People between the age of 16-30 years old
Describe your business model
Use of advertisements to monetize the system and Premium Subscription to remove ads

List features for current + future versions

  • Different categories
  • Each category has 10 questions every day/week
  • Ad before showing the result
  • Dynamic sound track
  • Sharing button
  • Show results for friends, country, city
  • Sharable questions and answers
  • Be able to choose an answer
  • Allow users to create questions for later review
  • A flag of inappropriate content for users
  • Option for creating an account
  • Show the % of people that answered each option
  • Randomized questions by category

Purpose features of our MVP

  • Different categories
  • Sharing button for social media
  • Be able to choose an answer
  • Option for creating an account
  • Show the % of people that answered each option
  • Randomized questions by category
  • Basic navigation
